Valentine's Chocolate Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 ¼ teaspoons baking powder
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup butter, softened
  • 1 ¼ cups white sugar
  • 1 egg

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 190ºC(375ºF).

  2. Sift together the flour, cocoa, baking powder and salt; set aside.

  3. In a medium bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until smooth.

  4. Beat in the egg. Gradually stir in the sifted ingredients to form a soft dough.

  5. Divide dough into 2 pieces, flatten and wrap in plastic wrap. Refrigerate for 2 hours.

  6. On a lightly floured surface, roll the dough out to 1/8 inches in thickness.

  7. Cut into desired shapes with cookie cutters.

  8. Dough will be sticky so be sure to add more flour to the rolling surface as needed.

  9. Place cookies 1 1/2 inches apart onto cookie sheets.

  10. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes in the preheated oven.

  11. Allow cookies to cool on baking sheet for 5 minutes before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.
